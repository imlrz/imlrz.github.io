01About

I'm Ruizhe Li (李睿哲), an undergraduate at USTC majoring in Data Science. My research asks how LLM-based agents can perform deep research — searching, reasoning over evidence, and writing reports at expert level — and how they should remember across long horizons. I care about rigorous, verifiable evaluation: most of what we claim about agents is only as good as the yardstick we measure it with.

DeepResearch Bench II: Diagnosing Deep Research Agents via Rubrics from Expert Reports

Ruizhe Li*, Mingxuan Du*, Benfeng Xu, Chiwei Zhu, Xiaorui Wang, Zhendong Mao · *equal contribution

arXiv:2601.08536 · 2026

A benchmark of 132 expert-grounded tasks across 22 domains and 9,430 fully-verifiable binary rubrics, scoring deep-research reports on information recall, analysis, and presentation. Built with a four-stage LLM+human pipeline and 400+ expert-hours — even the strongest systems satisfy fewer than 50% of rubrics.
